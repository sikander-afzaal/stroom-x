What is Stroomx

The StroomX eLearning approach is imaginative, multi sensory and fun, creating a dynamic experience for children. Our proprietary building kits, bundled with presentations, model plans & worksheets have been designed by architects, engineers and academicians to deliver hands on lessonscorrelated to cross disciplinary objectives that follow educational standards. The focus continues on concepts of STEAM (science, technology, engineering, arts & mathematics) for children.
